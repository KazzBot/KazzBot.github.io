Stupendous Stew

  • Time: One week or so
  • Serves: Entirety of the Luncheon Kingdom
Ingredients Quantity
Giant Meat One Colossal Piece
Lunchy Munchy Greens 50 Giant Heads
Bubbling Broth 100 Scoops

Step 1: Pour the bubbling broth, which can be collected from the
scalding lakes around the Luncheon Kingdom, into the ginormous pot.
Step 2: Chop up the greens into whatever size suits you, and throw them into the pot.
Step 3: Cook the meat for 5 minutes over the pot.
Step 4: Toss the meat into the stew.
Step 5: Wait for a week... and your stew is ready to eat!

Sparkle Water

  • Time: As long as it takes you to collect water
  • Serves: Depends on who you're serving
Ingredients Quantity
Sparkling Water 100 Scoops

Step 1: Get 100 scoops of sparkling water from the Seaside Kingdom. And that's it!

Salty Seaweed

  • Time: 30 Minutes if you're a water creature, an hour or longer for everyone else
  • Serves: 10-12 people
Ingredients Quantity
Living seaweed from the lake kingdom 20 Pieces
Salt 3 Tablespoons

Step 1: Go to the lake kingdom underwater passages.
Step 2: Kill 20 Living seaweed and being them back up to your cooking area.
Step 3: Lay them out on a surface and sprinkle the three tablespoons of salt on them.
Step 4: Cook them for 20 minutes, and they will be ready.

And there you have it! Three genuine Odyssey recipes! Enjoy!
